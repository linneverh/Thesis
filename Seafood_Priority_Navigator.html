Where to begin with sustainability — as a seafood-processing SME

European seafood processors face a fragmented wall of sustainability demands — regulators, retail customers, certifiers, investors and NGOs, each asking for different things through different channels. The hard question is not whether to act, but where to start so that a single capability investment satisfies as many of those demands as possible.

This tool answers exactly that question. It is one part of a wider toolkit — here is where it sits:

Your own impacts on people and planet (inside-out) — a double-materiality assessment complements this demand-side view.
How to use it.  1 · Enter your company profile.  2 · Tick which demands actually apply to you — your certifications, customers and investors.  3 · See your build-first priority topics and the share of your demands they cover.  4 · Click any topic to see who demands it and the enabling instruments that help you build it.
What powers this. The staging — which topics are build-first — reflects the sector-wide demand landscape and does not change with your selection. The coverage % is personalised to the demands you tick. Both are derived from a cross-framework coverage matrix that maps 30+ regulatory, customer, investor and industry instruments onto ESRS topics on a binding/touched scale, with every score justified against a cited source (Phase 3 coverage matrix).
⚠ The ranking shows strategic priority across stakeholders — it is not a legal-compliance checklist. Applicable law applies regardless of where a topic lands. Use this as a starting point, not a complete plan.
